Hello! My name is Rebecca!

I also go by Kara.

I have been drawing animals since elementary school. I have never been professionally trained, but the more I drew the better I got. I have always loved drawing canines, but I can do just about any animal with a few references. I do art as a side job to fund my Dog Sports passion. Along with art, I have always loved dog training ever since we got a puppy when I was 13. I now compete with my dogs doing sports such as Obedience, Rally, Scentwork, Fast CAT, and Agility. I am hoping to compete eventually in Dock Diving, Barn Hunt, Flyball, and Herding. I like to try a little bit of everything with my Italian Greyhound and Rottweiler!
