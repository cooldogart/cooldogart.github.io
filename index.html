Hello! My name is Rebecca!

I also go by Kara for all things art.

I have been drawing animals since I was a child. I have never been professionally trained, but I picked up tips and tricks throughout the years and developed my style on my own. I have always loved drawing canines, but I can do just about any animal with a few references! I do art as a side job to fund my Dog Sports passion. I now compete with my current dogs doing sports such as Obedience, Rally, Scentwork, Agility, and Flyball. We have hopes to earn future titles in Dock Diving, Barn Hunt, Fast CAT, and Herding. It is fun to try a little bit of everything with my Italian Greyhound and Rottweiler!
